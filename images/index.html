Blueberry Cake

The blueberry recipe below produces a cake that is light and fresh, bursting with flavor that captures the essence of the season. It’s exactly the dessert I waited for all winter.


Butterscotch Cake

This Butterscotch Cake is a moist, from-scratch brown sugar cake topped with butterscotch buttercream made with melted butterscotch chips! The cake is covered in even more butterscotch chips!

Choco-Truffle Cake

Chocolate truffles are a deeply indulgent chocolate treat made from chocolate and cream. The cake itself is dense and moist, just like proper chocolate cake should be, and the frosting.


Blackforest Cake

This deliciously moist Black Forest Cake is a cut above the rest with homemade whipped cream, rich chocolate ganache, and sweet spiked cherries. It has a taste of Butterscotch cum Chocolate taste.


Coconut Angel Cake

This airy cake is as light as can be, but the toasted almonds ensure it's big on flavor. The airy layers of this sweet cake are fluffy and delicious. This soaring cake is big on flavor but lighter on fat and sugar thanks to its airy triple layers.


Pineapple Cake

This Pineapple Cake recipe is made with crushed pineapple and topped with cream cheese whipped cream for a light but decadent treat! Pineapple cake is a sweet pastry containing butter, flour, egg, sugar, and pineapple jam or slices.
